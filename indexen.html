Welcome to the Raków Częstochowa website!

RKS Raków Częstochowa is a sports club from Częstochowa, founded in 1921 under the name Racovia. Its name comes from the district of Raków, where the club is based. Since the 2019/20 season, the men's football team has been competing in the Ekstraklasa, the top tier of Polish football. They were crowned Polish champions in 2023, twice runners-up in the Polish league, and winners of the Polish Cup and Polish Super Cup in 2021 and 2022. ... In the 2022/2023 season, Raków reached the final of the Polish Cup for the third consecutive time. After a goalless draw, they lost to Legia Warsaw 5-6 in a penalty shootout. On May 7, 2023, Pogoń Szczecin won 2-1 against Legia Warsaw on their home ground. This result secured Raków their first-ever Polish championship three rounds before the end of the season. During the Ekstraklasa Gala summarizing the 2022/2023 season, Vladan Kovačević was chosen as the goalkeeper of the season for the second year in a row, Fran Tudor received the title of defender of the season, and Marek Papszun was named the coach of the season.
